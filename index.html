As Developer Freelance 2008

I worked creating custom applications for Desktop, mobile devices and web for companies, especially in the field of education and management of educational centers. Since 2009 I have started the development of assistance tools for children with disabilities to be included in a conventional classroom. Coding in VB.Net, C#, Php, Javascript, Python.

As IT Manager 2009 - 2014

I was in charge of a team of 8 people, I started by managing the accuracy and integrity of the hardware and software applications for the Educational Centers Support project through Free Software tools in towns with extreme poverty. I managed the budgets of my department and worked together with the development team to ensure the adequate results of our software and our resources. Work with VB.Net, C#, Php, Javascript, Python, Ruby, Illustrator, Photoshop, Muse, Blender, Cinema 4d

As CTO / Project Manager 2015 - 2018

I was in charge of a team of 15 professionals in the design and development of applications for the project of insertion of people with disabilities in the university educational system, a pilot project in Costa Rica with projection for Latin America. I managed the technical and budget resources, I got involved with the design of the project and the User Experience (UX). I was part of the strategic management and established a constant dialogue with the General Management. Work with VB.Net, C#, Php, Javascript, Python, Illustrator, Photoshop, Muse, Sketch, XD, Raspberry Pi, Arduino.

As Founder 2019 - 2022

After more than 10 years of experience developing and designing technological solutions, I decided to implement my knowledge in my own projects, promoting my vision of developing applications in favor of minorities in my country. I work on the development of urban prototypes of citizen assistance for blind and deaf people. I am working on the creation of an application that measures the consumption of non-recyclable materials in the textile industry and establishes solid information to develop alternatives that are eco-friendly. I coordinate the design and development team and establish business relationships with the local government to promote educational applications based on local networks for areas where there is no internet access Work with VB.Net, C#, Php, Javascript, Python, Illustrator, Photoshop, Muse, Sketch, XD, Raspberry Pi, Arduino.
